Seafood Processing

Our modern facilities and skilled staff maintain quality from intake to dispatch.

  1. Receive Raw Materials: From verified, sustainable suppliers.
  2. Inspect Quality: Thorough checks for freshness, size, and safety.
  3. Sort & Grade: Categorised by size, weight, and quality.
  4. Freeze or Chill: Rapid cooling to optimal temperatures.
  5. Clean & Cut: Filleting and trimming to customer specs.
  6. Package: Secure, attractive packaging for transit.
  7. Label & Dispatch: Accurate labels and fast, cold‑chain delivery.
